Overview
Vegvisr is a mobile app for creating, organizing, and sharing knowledge graphs as concise notes. It helps users capture ideas, structure information with headings and bullets, and enrich content with AI-assisted drafting, images, and voice input.

Key Features
- Knowledge graph notes: create and edit graph-based notes with titles, structured Markdown, and optional YouTube links.
- AI Assistant: draft or refine content using AI (Gemini or OpenAI) and insert responses directly into your graph.
- AI image generation: generate images from prompts and insert them into notes.
- Image analysis (OpenAI): analyze a selected image and add the description to your graph.
- Voice input: record speech, transcribe it, and turn it into a draft message or insert it into notes.
- Sharing: open graphs in a browser for public viewing.

How It Works
1) Sign in with your phone number.
2) Choose or create a knowledge graph.
3) Use the AI Assistant to draft or improve content.
4) Generate or analyze images if needed.
5) Save and share your graph.

AI Providers
- Gemini (Firebase AI): text generation and image generation (Imagen).
- OpenAI: text generation, image generation, image analysis, and audio transcription.

Data and Privacy Summary
The app processes and stores the content you create (notes, AI prompts, images, and voice input) to provide core features.
Data is sent to third-party providers only to deliver selected features:
- Firebase (notifications and related services).
- OpenAI or Gemini for AI functionality.
- Storage/CDN for uploaded images and audio.

You control what content you create and share. For full details, refer to the privacy policy.

Permissions Used
- Microphone (for voice input).
- File access (for selecting images and audio).
- Notifications (for push messaging).
